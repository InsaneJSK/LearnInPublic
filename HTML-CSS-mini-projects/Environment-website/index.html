The Earth is what we all have in common.

~Wendell Berry

We, at NaturePreservators.org intend to preserve our Mother Earth from all types of destruction and varieties of pollution. We understand that we cannot do it by ourselves and would need help and support in various forms from people around us. You can reach us and contribute in preservation of earth by joining us on NaturePreservators.org. Your support can be by joining us as a member and creating awareness in the society on the importance of nature preservation. Members try and stop ongoing or regular destruction done to our environment by informing the concerned authorities and concerned people so that the corrective action can be taken. We urge all people to inform us on any nature/environment damaging activities by bringing it to the light of respective authorites through inform us page.
