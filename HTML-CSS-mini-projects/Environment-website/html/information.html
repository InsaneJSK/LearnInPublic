Information Box

Around us we find many people who don't care about the environment. We have no option but to mend their ways. For this purpose we have the information box. Here you can type your information regarding nature damage along with some personal details like mobile number name, etc. The information will be sent to us which we will forward to the required organisation and the action will be taken as soon as possible. Informations may be on the following topics or more:-

  1. Mass cutting of trees without permission.
  2. Industries near the residencial areas.
  3. Improper waste dumping.
